How it works

1. Ingest signals

Browser events, security events, integrity telemetry, and anomaly candidates.

2. Score trust

Hourly trust scoring per path. Factors explain what moved the needle.

3. Prescribe action

Playbooks and presets matched to stack context and incident type.

4. Verify recovery

Automated checks show impact reduction and recovery evidence.
